Join the Crew

Help us chart the course of campus conversation. The Argosy is seeking passionate students to join our team.

Why Join The Argosy?

Build Your Portfolio

Gain real-world experience and publish work that gets seen. Perfect for aspiring journalists, writers, and media professionals.

Join a Community

Work with a diverse team of creative and driven students who share your passion for storytelling and truth-seeking.

Make an Impact

Your work will inform campus discourse, hold institutions accountable, and contribute to a more engaged community.

Open Positions

Staff Writer / Reporter

Cover a beat you're passionate about, from campus politics to the local arts scene. We're looking for curious, diligent reporters who can find and tell compelling stories. No prior experience necessary, just a strong writing voice.

Photographer / Videographer

Capture the moments that define campus life. We need visual storytellers who can produce high-quality photos and videos to accompany our articles. Must have your own equipment.

Copy Editor

Ensure every article we publish is clear, accurate, and grammatically correct. A sharp eye for detail and a love for language are essential. This is a vital role for maintaining our quality standards.

How to Apply

Ready to come aboard? Please review our minimum requirements before applying:

  • A current resume.
  • Two relevant work samples (e.g., articles, photographs, design work).
  • A minimum of two semesters of experience on a school newspaper or similar publication.

If you meet these qualifications, please send your application materials to the address below. In your email, tell us which position you're interested in and why you'd be a great fit for The Argosy.

join@theargosy.com
